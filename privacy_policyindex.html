🛡️ DeIDGuard – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Version 1.1.0  ·  Chrome Extension

🔒 DeIDGuard processes ALL text locally on your device. No data is ever transmitted to any server, API, or third party — not even anonymized or aggregated data. This is an unconditional guarantee.

1. Overview

DeIDGuard is a Chrome browser extension designed to help users locally de-identify sensitive text before sharing it with AI tools. This Privacy Policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it is used, and what is never done with it.

This policy applies to DeIDGuard version 1.0.0 and above, distributed via the Chrome Web Store.

3. Permissions Justification

DeIDGuard requests only the minimum permissions required:

  • activeTab — Required to read the user's selected text from the currently active browser tab when the user explicitly triggers the extension. Only accessed on-demand; never passively monitoring.
  • scripting — Required to inject a minimal script that captures the current text selection. The injected script only reads window.getSelection().toString() and returns it to the extension.
  • storage — Required to save settings, history, and custom rules to chrome.storage.local on the user's device. No data is saved to chrome.storage.sync (cloud sync) — local only.
  • sidePanel — Required to open the DeIDGuard interface as a persistent side panel alongside the browser window, providing a superior workflow experience for privacy-conscious professionals.

DeIDGuard does not request: host_permissions, webRequest, cookies, identity, geolocation, or any other permission beyond the four listed above.

4. Local Storage

The following data is stored using chrome.storage.local on your device only:

  • Your settings (review mode, label preference, date granularity)
  • Your de-identification history (last 5 entries, capped automatically)
  • Your custom rule packs (if you create them)
  • Seeded rule packs (Basic and Healthcare Pro)

This data never leaves your device. It is not synchronized to Google's servers via chrome.storage.sync. You can clear all stored data at any time by clicking "Clear All History" in the History panel or by removing the extension.

5. What DeIDGuard Does NOT Do

  • Does not transmit any text — selected, cleaned, or partial — to any external server or API
  • Does not use any third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • Does not load any remotely-hosted code or scripts
  • Does not use cloud storage or sync user data
  • Does not auto-fill or auto-submit text to AI services — the "Open Claude" and "Open ChatGPT" buttons only open a new tab to those websites
  • Does not access tabs that are not the currently active tab
  • Does not run on chrome:// or other browser system pages
  • Does not collect identifiers, device fingerprints, or system information

6. Important Disclaimers

DeIDGuard is a privacy-assistance tool, not a legal compliance product.

  • DeIDGuard does not guarantee HIPAA compliance or Safe Harbor de-identification status under 45 CFR §164.514(b).
  • DeIDGuard does not provide legal advice of any kind.
  • The rule-based review feature is a pattern-matching assistant only — it does not constitute regulatory review or legal counsel.
  • Final review of all de-identified text before sharing remains the sole responsibility of the user.
  • Users in regulated industries (healthcare, legal, finance) should consult qualified legal and compliance professionals regarding their specific obligations.

7. Children's Privacy

DeIDGuard is intended for professional use by adults. It does not knowingly collect any information from individuals under 13 years of age.
