FAQ

Questions answered.

Does DeIDGuard make my workflow HIPAA compliant?

No. DeIDGuard is a de-identification assistance tool. It automates the mechanical removal of identifiers but does not constitute HIPAA compliance, legal advice, or certification. Your organization's compliance posture depends on many factors beyond a single tool. Always consult your privacy officer and legal counsel.

Does any data get sent anywhere?

No. DeIDGuard has no server, no API, and no cloud component. All processing happens in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing leaves your device. You can verify this by inspecting the extension's source code — it makes zero network requests with your text.

Does it work with any AI tool?

Yes. DeIDGuard copies the clean text to your clipboard. You then paste it into Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any other AI tool manually. The quick-open buttons open Claude and ChatGPT in a new tab for convenience, but the actual pasting is always manual — by design.

What is the Re-Identification Risk Score?

Removing direct identifiers (names, SSNs, etc.) is necessary but not always sufficient. Quasi-identifiers — age, gender, specific diagnosis, sub-state geography, occupation — can still allow re-identification through combination. DeIDGuard scores the residual risk after de-identification and lists which quasi-identifier factors remain for your review.

Is it free?

Yes. The core extension is free and complete. Future Pro features for teams and enterprise use are planned. The free version will always exist.

What browsers does it support?

DeIDGuard is a Chrome extension built on Manifest V3. It works in Google Chrome and any Chromium-based browser (Edge, Brave, Arc) that supports Chrome extensions.
